Partnership

MuguPay Reward Partner

MuguPay offers comprehensive technology platform & ecosystem for participating reward partners to drive their customer loyalty & promotion programs. You can partner with MuguPay as:

MuguPay Rewards Partner (MRP):

MuguPay Rewards Partner can run their Customer Loyalty Program by offering MuguPay points to their regular customers as promotions. For example, spend Rs. 500/- & get 20 mugu points. You can design multiple dynamic promotions like get 2x points for spending between 2-5 PM. Reward partners can also offer freebies, discounts, buy one get one etc offers using MuguPay customer engagement program & share with MuguPay customers in mobile app or social media.

Read More

MuguPay Redemption Partner (MRP):

MuguPay Redemption Partner allows MuguPay customers to redeem their MuguPay points against sales of products & services. Redemption Partner don’t have to pay anything extra for accepting MuguPay points. Merchants can redeem MuguPay points to equivalent amount.

MuguPay Partner Plus (MPP):

MuguPay Partner Plus are merchants who provide rewards as well as redemption services to MuguPay customers. MuguPay Partner Plus will receive exclusive promotion platforms from MuguPay & Mugu.shop & redemption marketplace, Payment POS terminals, ERP Solutions, Access to Supply Chain Financing etc.
