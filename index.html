What is Mugupay?

MuguPay is a unified customer loyalty & reward program – the first of its kind and the largest in Nepal. It is designed in such a way that all kinds of businesses, enterprises, corporate houses, supermarkets, MSMEs can be part of MuguPay and launch their loyalty programs without any technical dependencies.
